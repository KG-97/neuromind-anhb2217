# Launch Checklist

## Positioning

Use this message:

> NeuroMind helps ANHB2217 students study faster with workbook-style revision, lesion logic, quick simulations, and AI explanations.

Do not lead with “AI-powered platform.” That phrase has been beaten to death and buried.

## Soft launch

- [ ] share with a few ANHB2217 classmates
- [ ] send the Lab 5 workbook first
- [ ] ask one question only: “What confused you or slowed you down?”
- [ ] fix friction before shouting about it publicly

## First public release

- [ ] dashboard live
- [ ] Lab 5 workbook linked clearly
- [ ] README reflects reality
- [ ] deployment guide exists
- [ ] at least one feedback channel is visible

## Feedback prompts

Use prompts like:

- “Which part saved you time?”
- “Which section felt confusing or too long?”
- “What topic should be the next workbook?”
